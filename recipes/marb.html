Marbled Chcolate Mud Cake

Ingredients

The Cake

  • 250g butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cups (275g) caster sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 1/4 cups (335g) self-raising flour
  • 3/4 cup (180ml) buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup (25g) cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) milk
  • 1/2 cup (95g) white Choc Bits
  • 1/2 cup (95g) dark Choc Bits

The Chocolate Butter Cream

  • 125g butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups (240g) icing sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder

The Steps

  1. Preheat oven to moderate (180°C/160°C fan-forced. Grease deep 22cm round cake pan; line base with baking paper.
  2. Beat butter, extract and sugar in small bowl with electric mixer until light and fully. Add eggs, one at a time, beating until just comnined between aditions; transfer mixture to a large bowl. Stir in sifted flour and buttermilk, in two batches.
  3. Divide cake mixture between two bowls. Blend sifted cocoa with milk; stir into one of the bowls of mixture with White Choc Bits. Stir dark Choc Bits into remainig mixture.
  4. Drop alternate spoonfuls of mixtures into a pan, then bull a skewer back and forth through the cake micture several times to achieve a marbled effect. Bake cake for about 1 hour. Stand for 5 minutes; turn, top side up, onto wire rack to cool.
  5. Meanwhile, make chocolate butter cream.
  6. Drop alternate spoonfuls of the two butter cream micturesonto cake; spread over top and side of cake.

The Butter Cream

Beat butter in small bowl with electric mixer until light and fluffy. Gradually beat in half of the icing sugar, then the milk, then the remaining icing sugar. Transfer half of the mixture to another small bowl; stir sifted cocoa into one of the bowls
